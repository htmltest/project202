Transport hubs in Moscow

One of the main ways in which Moscow's transport infrastructure is developing is through the creation of a system of transport hubs (TH). The THs connect underground and surface public transport into a unified network, allowing passengers to quickly and safely transfer between modes of transport. Well-placed crosswalks protect passengers from mishaps during transfer between different modes of transport.

The hubs are designed with the particular transport situation of each district in mind and help riders easily transfer from their cars to the subway or buses.

The comfort of the citizenry is predicated upon a safe and accessible urban environment.
